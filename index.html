Why Us?

At the Center for Developmental Pediatrics, we are dedicated to assessment, diagnosis and treatment of children with special needs and developmental disabilities. Dr Rajpal, believes that with early intervention there is tremendous hope for children with difficulties, due to plasticity of the brain before age 6yrs. We believe that any child can learn if taught in tiny steps and using his preferred style of learning .


We provide not only assessment and diagnoses, but also an individual plan for each child. Based on the IEP(Individual Education Program),each child is given a home program, which is a work guideline for parents.As the chil d progresses, subsequent programs are given to the parent.


In addition ,reccomendations are made for speech therapy,occupational therapy,special education,physical therapy and social skills training, depending on the individual needs of the child.
