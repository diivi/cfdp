Parenting a child with special needs.


Understanding and accepting the diagnosis


As a pediatrician who sees a lot of children with Developmental challenges’ and Special needs, I have come to understand the anguish parents feel, once they are given such a diagnosis.

The process of acceptance takes time and courage. However a positive, proactive approach, Early Intervention, intensive home program and medication in some cases; these have tremendous impact on the outcomeThe intellectual, social, emotional , language and academic progress is in your hands-Your goal, Maximizing the child’s potential - “Be the Best that she can be”.
