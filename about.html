About

Dr Radha Rajpal M.D(Pediatrics)
Fellowship in Developmental Pediatrics(L.H.M.C)
Fellowship Royal College of Pediatrics and Child health (U.K)

With over ten years experience in Developmental Pediatrics and eighteen years as a pediatrician, Dr Radha Rajpal has transformed the lives of hundreds of children with developmental challenges. She is passionate about helping children with special needs and their families. With a deep affinity for developmentally challenged children even as a young Pediatrician.,she believed that all children have tremendous potential and deserve the opportunity to reach it.


Dr Rajpal,graduated with an M.B.B.S from Lady Hardinge Medical College,New Delhi and M.D in Pediatrics from University of Mumbai. She received a Fellowship with the Royal College of Pediatrics and Child Health, U.K.in 2004.In 2011 she did a Fellowship in Developmental Pediatrics at her Alma mater,Lady Hardinge Medical College and Kalawati Saran Childrenâ€™s Hospital.She has attended training workshops on Applied Behavioral Analysis therapy and Floor time therapy and trains parents in the same.


She has rich experience in the area of Development, having worked with hospitals the world over. Locally she has consulted with premier hospitals in Gurgaon before starting an independent practice. In the past ten years she has consulted at the Developmental Clinic at Max hospital, Paras hospital and W- Pratiksha hospital.She has conducted talks, seminars and outreach in several schools in Gurgaon and New Delhi.


Dr Rajpal provides not only assessment and diagnosis but an action plan based on developmental goals.Achieving developmental goals in developmentally challenged children may need as much as 24-26 hours per week of therapy.Since so many hours of therapy is unaffordable and impractical .She trains parents to become the primary therapists, using the Eclectic approach ( a combination of floor time and ABA techniques.
